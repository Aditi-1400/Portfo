Dear India,

Dear India,
I Love You!
Despite all those garbage heaps and those twelve feet wide pits, or clogged drains,
there's something about this place that makes me fall in love with it all over again.

Maybe it's the soil, that has me attached to it by the roots.
Or is it narcotic winds that call out to me wherever I go?
Maybe it's those lush green fields with their typical scent.
Or the heat of the sun and flurry of rain pattered against the window.

Maybe it's that early morning echoes of temple bells.
I think, it's the beautiful Himalayas and the beaches of Goa.
Maybe the typical "Chuk Chuk" of those metal wheels on rusted rails?
No,it's that never ending horn from vehicle trails.

Maybe it's the faint fragrance of street food at every corner.
Perhaps, it's the change in ambiance with every mile I travel.
Maybe it's the way things work...erratic and unpredictable.
Oh wait! Is it those colorful celebrations loaded with drama?

Maybe it's the outright blunt, chaotic... yet, real people,
With culture rooted deep in their veins and the will to share!

Maybe...it's nothing fancy,
I don't know,
But there's something about this place,
That makes me fall in love each day, all over again,
Something that makes me wanna "stay".
~Haze
