Outside.
Anything to fill the growing hollow we don't understand.
Without understanding it's shape... It's size... It's need ,
Seeking numbness for comfort
Attention for love
Drama for meaning
But have we asked? Have we asked the deepening need , what it needs?
Maybe the answers are all within.
Maybe the hollow is the voice of the soul,imploring you... That it's time... It's time to seek

What you don't find in your solo trip

It's time to seek
but this time,
Within.
