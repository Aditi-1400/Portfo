The Truth Bleeds

The truth bleeds
And the love scars
But the lies feel sweet on my tongue
And smiles fill in all the cracks,
Sealing everything within.

The night laughs
As the silence scratches with it's sharp claws,
all the wounds now resurfacing.
But my days are sweet sedatives
Numbing everything with pretend.

My fear is my mentor,
Always making decisions, so I can take a back seat in my own journey.
And jealousy my old friend,
Hugs me close as I suffocate in it's embrace.

The memories haunt
And the future taunts
As my present tries to bind my scattered life
With ink and paper.
~Smoke.
