Monsters

Yes, monsters are real!
As real as you are and as real as I am!
And Oh! No, No, they don't live in fictional novels or movies, they live right here...along us!
And you know the irony - they don't even try to hide, still, we choose to ignore them.
Monsters
They instill the very basic human emotion - FEAR!
I'll tell you how it happens.
It begins,
When you can see pairs of eyes analyzing you from top to bottom and bottom to top;the vicious cycle goes... On and on and on!
And then you hear sounds of feet stalking you, some faint whistles even, Like a beast ready to curb it's hunger by ripping you into pieces.
And then horror strikes when your phone rings at 2 am, but...no one speaks; the kind which doesn't let you sleep no matter how many lullabies you hear!
Oh wait!
The monster hasn't reached you yet, It's still at a distance of 10 feet...still sneaking...still scaring!
And really no one cares to pay attention, Until that 10 feet distance is crossed.
And then the monster grabs you... Tearing you into shreds!
The remnants of this disaster are often seen on hospital beds...on burnt faces and sometimes, Huh! On those photos on walls.
~Haze
